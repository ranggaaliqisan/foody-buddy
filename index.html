What is FoodyBuddy

FoodyBuddy is Indonesia's innovative social food delivery platform that bridges the gap between food lovers, restaurants, and industry professionals. Inspired by the connectivity of professional networks like LinkedIn, we've created a unique ecosystem where you can follow and engage with official restaurant accounts, explore career opportunities in the food and beverage sector, and seamlessly order from your favorite establishments.

Our platform transforms the traditional food delivery experience into a vibrant community hub. Whether you're a passionate foodie looking to discover new culinary experiences, a professional seeking opportunities in the F&B industry, or a restaurant wanting to build stronger connections with your customers. FoodyBuddy brings everyone together in one integrated platform.
